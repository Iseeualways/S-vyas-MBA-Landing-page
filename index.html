About
S-VYASA Deemed-to-be University
Sattva Global City Campus, Kengeri, Bengaluru

Swami Vivekananda Yoga Anusandhana Samsthana, popularly known as S-VYASA University, is a UGC-approved Deemed-to-be University. The institution adopts a comprehensive approach to delivering quality education and training, while actively advancing research across a diverse range of disciplines.

The School of Advanced Studies at S-VYASA University is committed to instilling core values that help shape individuals into future-ready professionals. With NAAC A+ accreditation and AICTE approval, the university offers a technology-driven learning environment designed to prepare students for the global digital economy through direct industry exposure and immersive learning experiences.

S-VYASA University has two campuses in Bangalore. The Global City Campus is located on Mysore Road in Rajarajeshwari Nagar, while the Prashanti Kutiram Campus is situated in Anekal, near Jigani.

The MBA program at S-VYASA University is a full-time, two-year postgraduate course.

The university offers a wide range of specialisations across the MBA PRO Specialisations and the Dual Specialisations variations. Students can get in touch to understand which suits them the best.

The admission process is simple and guided. You can submit your enquiry through the form, connect with a counsellor, choose your preferred course, and complete the application with step-by-step assistance.

The program focuses on practical learning through internships, industry projects, and real-world exposure. With strong industry collaborations and a career-oriented approach, students are prepared to step confidently into professional roles.
